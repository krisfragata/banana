For queer interactions,
the line between platonic and romantic can be hazy
with relationships sometimes dipping in and out of the sexual.
Banana Split is an exploration of these queer dynamics,
its inherent lawlessness yet need for communication.
I’m coining the term “Banana Split,”
a situation in which no communication happens
thus creating ambiguity.

I think a lot of us have experienced something like
this before, queer or otherwise.
You meet someone new and things are fun
but y’all haven’t talked at all about intentions, expectations.
One person says, “I don’t want to keep doing this”
and the other says, “Okay, valid but wait…what was ~this~?
- I thought we were friends, would you like to end that too?”
Really, it ain't too deep.
Sometimes, the banana just splits :p ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
